The Penn-Del-Jersey Chapter of the National Electrical Contractors Association is a resource for electrical contractors in the Southeastern Pennsylvania, New Jersey and Delaware Areas. Here you will find the latest information related to the electrical industry.

2013 NECA Convention and Trade Show

The 2013 NECA Convention and Trade Show will be held in Washington, D.C., October 12 - October 15, 2013. The Convention Brochure contains information about the educational, as well as networking and business opportunities. In the back of the brochure, you will find the registration form. Please make sure that you complete all parts, including the housing section. Our Chapter was assigned the Renaissance Hotel. You may return the form by mail or fax or you may also register online, www.necaconvention.org. The deadline for early registration is September 10, 2013.

If you have any questions regarding your registration, please feel free to call the Chapter Office.
